<P><B>Know Your Stalker -- Mr. H</B>
<P>
<P>This mysterious enemy suddenly dropped from a helicopter. At first glance, it appears human, but it can withstand being dropped from great heights, or sprayed with gunfire.
<P>
<P>It also appears to act with an objective, however, its purpose is unclear, it has overwhelming combat capabilities, and with the exception of its unprotected head, its body is nearly impervious to small arms fire.
